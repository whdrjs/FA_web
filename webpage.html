Fridge Angle






냉장고의 요정 웹페이지


Idea Motivation



Nowadays many foods are wasted. Almost 30% of wastes are food wastes.

The current structure of Gachon university is the result of a merger between four existing universities.

Also many food wastes are wasted because of expiration date.

So our team will develop refrigerator management application







Key Feature



1. Manage foods' expiration date in refrigerator.


2. Lead users to throw away expired food..


3. Recommend food recipe with the food before expiration date..


4. Only make application available when the users are in their house (with GPS).


5. Use Database to store information about registered food.
